# Design Engineering Quote Library

A curated collection of quotes for use throughout the d×e course content. These aren't decoration—they're pedagogical anchors that connect students to the rich tradition of visual thinking.

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Format**: Always use `> *"Quote text."* — Attribution`
2. **Limit**: 2-3 quotes per lesson maximum
3. **Placement**: Opening (after intro), mid-lesson transitions, or closing inspiration
4. **Variety**: Mix Swiss masters with broader design voices
